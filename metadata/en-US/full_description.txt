A minimal Pomodoro app with special focus on task notes.

<b>Pomodoro + Timetracking = BeyondPomodoro</b>
1. Break down a large task into timed Pomodoro sessions
2. Write down your goal for the session
3. At the end of the session, you can <b>choose</b> to save the session to your calendar
4. You can also add end of session notes about how the session went

<b>Multiple activities</b>
* Maintain different work time, break time for each activity
* DND preferences for each activity

<b>Privacy focused</b>
* Your data lives in your calendar, and nowhere else
* The app never connects to the internet ever
* Open source, of course
